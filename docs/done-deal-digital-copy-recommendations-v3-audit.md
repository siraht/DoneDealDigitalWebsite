- [x] Generate five strong alternatives for each meaningful copy element group

## Scope Note

I grouped copy into meaningful decision surfaces: metadata, hero copy, CTA labels, support bullets, section headlines, grouped card copy, grouped FAQ sets, footer copy, and legal-page framing. I did not generate creative variants for full legal boilerplate paragraphs because those need factual rewrites tied to real operations, processors, and contact details.

## Highest-Risk Current Copy Issues

1. The site repeatedly uses hype language the plan explicitly rejects: `dominate`, `first page of Google`, `market leader`, `booked solid`, and similar claims.
2. The homepage does not answer the plan’s four core questions fast enough and does not clearly sequence Web Design as the primary entry offer.
3. Local SEO and Lead Generation both drift into promises or implied guarantees the plan says not to make.
4. The Case Studies route reads like public proof, but the plan says it should not be published until proof thresholds are met.
5. The legal pages are duplicated, possibly inaccurate, and operationally unsafe in their current form.
